Care Scheduler (Demo)

Manage scheduling appointments, contact information, as well as invoicing

Find the project on github. This is an open source project under an MIT style license.

Maximise Resource

Cut down on time spent setting up weekly rotas and invoicing with speed and efficiency. Eliminate the need for manually pairing clients (patients) and staff for hourly, daily and weekly appointments. Schedule your staff for any proximity.

Responsive Design

Use from your mobile device, access client information while on the move.

Customizable And Easy to Deploy

Built with robust and powerful frameworks - Angular 2 and Dropwizard for Java. Open Sourced, free to use by all. Adapt it to your organisation's requirements.

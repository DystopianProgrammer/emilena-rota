Information and help

The Frances Taylor Foundation Rota is designed to store customer and staff information, and has the ability to generate a rota from this information automatically. The information stored, includes personal details such as name, address and contact information.

Availability & working times are associated to each client and staff member respectively. With this information to hand, the application can best determine how and when to allocate field staff to clients in the community in order to provide the neccessary support based on availability.

The benefit that this provides, is that allocating staff support for clients is an arduous task. Automating the process can cut down on the time spent manually attempting to pair staff with clients for specific times. Providing a high-level overview also allows for easy amendments where necessary. It also provides a centralised place to store all necessary contact information such as telephone numbers, E-mail addreses and residential addresses. Rota entries can be marked as completed, and with this data, invoicing can be derived.

The following features are all supported (v1.0 - Current):

  1. The creation of staff members
  2. The updating of staff members and their information
  3. The creation of clients
  4. The updating of client information
  5. The association of person and availability times for given days of the week
  6. Generating a rota at any given time
  7. Versioning of a rota
  8. Adding manual entries to a generated rota (if required). This may be down to absense or annual leave
  9. The updating of rota entries for any required ad-hoc changes with regards to scheduling and availability
  10. The viewing of any clients or staff who have not been allocated for a support appointment

Version 1.1 will include invoice generation from completed appointments. This is due January 2017.

Technical:

  1. Secure connections over HTTPS
  2. Custom administration credentials for private access
  3. Personal information is privately stored in a secured database
  4. Information stored adheres to all scheduling of the Data Protection Act
  5. No information is used for any other purposes, such as marketing etc
  6. The site does not use cookies

Any new features, improvements or issues should be reported to developer or analyst. The contact details can be found in the footer of the site.
